## 3. Procedure

1. Go to `mySNU -> research administration / academic research -> bioethics (IRB)`. `I6`
2. Choose the general `new review` category. `Q3`
3. Enter researcher information and add the advisor as required for thesis research. `I3`
4. Make sure the research plan explicitly says:
   - the data are public YouTube materials,
   - there is no survey, interview, recruitment, or experiment,
   - identifiable faces/comments/account linkage create residual risk,
   - the project will control that risk through de-identification and restricted handling.
5. Attach the consent-waiver or consent-not-obtained explanation. `I3`, `I13`
6. Respond to any pre-review correction request on the same day. `I4`

- do not assume exemption just because the data are public
- do not leave ethics documents to the end
