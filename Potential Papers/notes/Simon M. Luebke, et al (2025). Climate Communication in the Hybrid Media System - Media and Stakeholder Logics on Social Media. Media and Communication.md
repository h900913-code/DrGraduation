## 핵심 인용 후보

- Using ChatGPT, the study analyzed YouTube comments from news channels that covered a contentious entertainment issue with multiple related events.
- The findings indicated that communication behaviors changed over time and manifested in diverse combinations.
- In addition, the study demonstrated that aware publics in the digital era were not merely passive; rather, they engaged in communication activities similar to active publics.
- Furthermore, it identified distinct communication behaviors associated with certain types of publics, indicating that public types also evolve dynamically across events.
